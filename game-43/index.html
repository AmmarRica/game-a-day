/// TEMPORAL FOOTBALL SYSTEM v1.983 ///

You have the power to REWIND TIME.

Arrow Keys / WASD - Move your QB
SPACE - Snap ball / Throw to receiver
R - REWIND TIME (3 per game)

Play 4 quarters of simplified football.
Use rewinds strategically on crucial plays!
WARNING: Excessive rewinds cause temporal anomalies.
Anomaly defenders cannot be blocked!
